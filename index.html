About Us

Your Trusted Technology Partner

Torrent Computer Technology LLC (TCT), established by Eng. Hadi Abu Jabal in 2021, leverages over 16 years of extensive professional and educational experience in both local and international markets.

The company specializes in providing tailored and dependable solutions to enterprises in the government and private sectors within the UAE.

TCT is recognized as one of the rapidly growing IT services and product engineering firms, uniquely equipped to offer a diverse range of innovative products and services.

With a commitment to excellence, TCT aspires to be regarded as one of the leading IT service providers, aiming to expand its presence throughout the Gulf region and beyond.
